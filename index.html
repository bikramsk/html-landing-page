Understanding the Stages of Hair Fall

At Dr Batra's®, we personalise treatments based on the severity of hair loss, ensuring maximum regrowth potential. Hair loss progresses in different stages:

Stage 1-2

Mild thinning, slight hairline recession

Stage 3-4

Visible bald patches, widening parting

Stage 5-7

Extensive balding, almost no regrowth
